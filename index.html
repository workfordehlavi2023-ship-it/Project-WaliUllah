Our Purpose

Mission

"

The mission of Project Wali Ullah Bookclub is to nurture a generation of Muslims deeply aware of the foundations, ideologies, and cultural currents of modern Western Civilization and its many "isms." Through serious reading, intellectual engagement, and civilizational consciousness, we aim to prepare Muslim youth to face the challenges of westernization with clarity, confidence, and faith. Inspired by the intellectual legacy of Shah Wali Ullah Dehlavi, we aspire to cultivate minds capable of reviving and shaping an authentic Islamic worldview and social order for the future.

Who We Are

About the Project

Project Wali Ullah Bookclub is an intellectual and educational initiative founded by Sayyed Abdul Quadir Samdani — a student of theology and modernity, and an enthusiast of the thought and legacy of Shah Wali Ullah Dehlavi.

The project seeks to revive meaningful reading culture among Muslim youth and spread the timeless civilizational, spiritual, and intellectual ideas of Shah Wali Ullah across the globe.

Through curated readings, discussions, and reflections, the Bookclub aims to connect tradition with contemporary realities — building a community rooted in knowledge, revival, and meaningful change.
